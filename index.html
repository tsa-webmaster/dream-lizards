DREAM LIZZARDS



Dream Lizzards formed in 2014, playing at bars and small venues alongside their daytime jobs. In the summer of 2016, they signed a deal with the DEF Music Record Label. They quickly gained popularity, and they got enough funds to start touring. In spring next year, they went on their first tour of America. With their fanbase accumulating, they went on a tour of the world the next summer. As of now, they are doing a small tour of Pennsylvania, which is their state of origin. Buy tickets
